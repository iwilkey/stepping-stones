What is "Threadfeed"?


Threadfeed is a public, virtual social experiment
open to anyone who wants to participate.

Threadfeed users can create threads to be voted on by other users.
If a user gets an overwhlemingly positive response to their thread,
it could be admitted into the threadweb.

The theory is that the threadweb will become this amalgamation
of everything that somehow connects us.

For example, a thread could be some of your favorite song lyrics,
an opinion over a policital occurance, a funny joke, a beautiful quote,
words of encouragement, thoughts on mental health,
any statement one can think of that can be reviewed by other users.

A thread could also reinforce to us what we tend to dislike as well:
Hate, prejudice, racism, bigotry, ect
In the event they happen, choose to reject them.

The voting process is simple: a random
thread will be pulled from a database and given to
you to be voted on. It's your job to answer honestly.
If it makes you feel good, vote otherwise, vote .

If this seems at all interesting to you, great!
If you would like to continue, it's free, lightweight, and noncommittal.

Sign up below and make your first thread!
Remember, it could be anything.
(If nothing comes to mind, just vote on other threads)

If you have any questions, concerns, or insights
feel free to email threadfeeddotcom@gmail.com.
